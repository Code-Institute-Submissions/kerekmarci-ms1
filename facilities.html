Our facilities

Our stunning workout space will take you on a truly immersive fitness journey with some of the very best instructors in London. Our goal was to bring everything in one place, no matter what your needs or level are. And we don't compromise on quality - what be bring here is truly world-class.

High-end machines

Take advantage of the fantastic range of Fitness Pro Machine ® equipment available in our gym area. We'll continue to introduce the latest equipment to our gym to keep your workout fresh and interesting. A dedicated indoor athletics area aimed to help improve functional strength, acceleration and endurance. Experience exercises classes packed full of energy and motivation.

Swimming pool

Want to make a splash? Whether you want to do laps, train for a triathlon or unwind after a hard training session, we have this amazing pool for you! Our gym boasts a 25m heated indoor swimming pool, sauna and steam room. Our gym also offesr expert swim tuition - classes run for 30–60 minutes and are available for all abilities – from total beginners to those just wanting to improve their technique.

Classes

If you’re looking for a studio with classes that are as challenging as they are fun, we are here for you. All fitness levels are welcome, whether you are a beginner and starting out your new journey, or advanced and have ambitious goals, we have the right class for you! Check out our classes timetable to see which class suits your needs best. Our passionate and expert trainers will make sure that the classes are always a lot of fun, therefore there's no better way to socialise and get fit at the same time!
